Our Academic Approach

Preparing Students for Success

At St. Mary's High School, we believe in educating the whole person. Our academic program combines rigorous coursework with opportunities for personal growth and character development.

With a student-teacher ratio of 12:1, our faculty provide individualized attention to help each student reach their full potential. Our graduates consistently gain admission to top universities and excel in their chosen fields.

Our curriculum emphasizes critical thinking, problem-solving, and real-world application of knowledge. Through project-based learning and interdisciplinary studies, students develop the skills needed for success in college and beyond.

Academic Departments

Mathematics

Our mathematics department offers a comprehensive curriculum from Algebra through Calculus, including AP and honors courses. Students develop strong problem-solving skills applicable to all fields of study.

  • Algebra I & II Grades 9-10
  • Geometry Grades 9-10
  • Pre-Calculus Grades 10-11
  • AP Calculus AB/BC Grades 11-12
  • Statistics Grades 11-12

Science

Our science program emphasizes hands-on learning in state-of-the-art laboratories. Students explore biology, chemistry, physics, and environmental science through inquiry-based instruction.

  • Biology Grade 9
  • Chemistry Grade 10
  • Physics Grade 11
  • AP Environmental Science Grades 11-12
  • Forensic Science Grades 11-12

English

Our English curriculum develops critical reading, writing, and communication skills. Students analyze literature from diverse cultures and time periods while honing their own writing abilities.

  • World Literature Grade 9
  • American Literature Grade 10
  • British Literature Grade 11
  • AP English Literature Grade 12
  • Creative Writing Grades 11-12

Social Studies

Our social studies program helps students understand historical contexts, cultural diversity, and civic responsibility. Courses emphasize analytical thinking and evidence-based argumentation.

  • World History Grade 9
  • U.S. History Grade 10
  • Government & Economics Grade 11
  • AP European History Grades 11-12
  • Psychology Grades 11-12

World Languages

Our language program develops proficiency in speaking, listening, reading, and writing while fostering cultural understanding. Many students achieve fluency by graduation.

  • Spanish I-IV Grades 9-12
  • French I-IV Grades 9-12
  • Mandarin Chinese I-IV Grades 9-12
  • Latin I-III Grades 9-12
  • AP Language Courses Grades 11-12
